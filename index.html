Instructions

  1. Keep your laptop's screen straight.
  2. Move yourself approximately 3-4 feet away from the laptop.
  3. Move your right wrist in an ascending and decending order, a red dot should appear on your right wrist.
  4. Press the play button. Again, move yourself 3-4 feet away from the laptop.
  5. The red paddle is your paddle, that will move as per the movement of your right wrist.
